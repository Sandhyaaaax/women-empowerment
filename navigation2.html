What We Do

Protection That Thinks For You

Six intelligent layers — working silently, continuously, so you never have to fumble in a moment of fear.

🗺️

SafePath™ Algorithm

Routes scored by Safety Density — lighting, open businesses, comfort ratings, and blind spot avoidance. Logic is classified to prevent misuse.

🧠

AI Distress Detection

On-device ML reads motion, inactivity, and accelerometer patterns — acting before you can even reach for your phone.

🚔

Police Auto-Sync

Pre-fetches the nearest precinct API by geofence sector. Distress = instant GPS, battery, and 30s audio transmitted automatically.

🕶️

Shadow / Camo Mode

Flip phone face-down or shake 3× — app disguises as a working calculator. Silent PIN checks continue every 60 seconds.

💬

Multilingual Chatbot

Hindi, Hinglish, English — voice or text. Support in the language you're most comfortable with, exactly when you need it.

🌸

Women's Health Hub

Period tracker, PCOD guidance, skincare community — one private, encrypted space for total wellbeing.

How It Works

Your Safety, Automated in 3 Steps

Set it once — SafePing handles everything else from there.

1

Quick Check-In

Set your expected safe duration. SafePing immediately begins silent background protection — GPS, AI, and police sync all armed.

2

SafePath™ Routes

App suggests the highest Safety Density route — not fastest, safest. Blind spots, darkness, and abandoned zones are automatically avoided.

3

Auto Alert + Sync

No response when time ends? Trusted contacts get your live GPS. Distress detected? Nearest police precinct is auto-notified instantly.
